What is the Admission Interview?

The Admission Interview is the final step in our application before getting accepted. Once you complete the Trials, you will get invited to complete a quick 20-30 minute interview with us. The interview will happen remotely and asynchronously through a video interview software so you can do it whenever it's most convenient for you.
